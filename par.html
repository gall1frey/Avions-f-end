achieve the unsurpassed. The team includes students from
multiple disciplines striving to achieve a common goal.

Upcoming Competitions

Aerophilia 2019

Aerophilia is a National Level Aeromodelling Event with more than 20 events, Organized by Team Challengers, Sahyadri College of Engineering and Management, Mangalore.

Boeing Aerotrix 2020

The Boeing National Aeromodelling Competition for college students in India is sponsored by Boeing, and conducted in collaboration with IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, IIT Kanpur, IIT Kharagpur and IIT Madras.

IAAA 2019

The purpose of the IAAA Aircraft Building Competition is to promote and develop Indian Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) at the university and college levels.

SAE CHENNAI 2020

The Aero Design Competition challenges participating teams to conceive, design, fabricate and test a radio controlled aircraft that can take off, land, and optimally meet the mission requirements.
